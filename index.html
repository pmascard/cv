Summary

I am a data management professional, with extensive experience of consolidating business operational data into, strategically and operationally aligned, consumable data repositories.

Role profiles have included:

  • Managerial - defining, building, restructuring, mentoring, and managing teams.
  • Analytical - business analysis, data analysis, data mapping, and process reengineering.
  • Development - data definition, manipulation, extraction, transforming and conforming.
  • Reporting - Semantic layer and reporting development.
  • Architectural - Data modelling, solution design and administration.
  • Governance - Data quality assessment, remediation, catalogues, policies, and frameworks.
  • Operational - Performance tuning, load process management and release management.

I am motivated by a desire to see organisations improve the effectiveness of data within their business processes, through sustainable practices that foster ownership, direction, and personal development within the team.

I am proficient in SQL and data development/transformation practices as well as being comfortable supporting the workflow using Microsoft Office, SharePoint, Confluence and Jira. I am always happy to improve skills and to learn new ones as the role demands.
